Rules

Welcome to SpectraLunar. Breaking the rules will result in being muted, kicked, or banned. Fundamentally.

  1. Be kind to other members.
    1. Trolling, impersonation, and similar acts are not acceptable!
    2. Follow common courtesy in voice chat. Keep it SFW, do not disturb in-progress games with off-topic chatter, and properly calibrate your microphone.
  2. No offensive/controversial/nsfw avatars, pictures, emotes, or topics. This means no politics or religion.
    1. If you believe this rule is being breached, please contact an available moderator. Do not take action on your own.
    2. Links to other Discord servers must be verified by a admin before being posted.
  3. Do not carelessly spoil important plot points of any media in general, regardless of its age.
  4. Shitposting, spamming, and low-effort memes are prohibited. Don’t use excessive unicode or emoji in usernames or text.
    1. If you believe this rule is being breached, please contact an available moderator. Do not take action on your own.

    All Moderator decisions are final.

    • Moderators have full discretion to remove content or users deemed unpleasant or unwelcomed by community members.
    • Moderators have full discretion to change channel topics.

    Respect Discord's Terms of Service.

    Abusing or exploiting bugs in Silva Bot or Discord itself is a bannable offense.

    If you'd like to appeal a ban you can Here.
