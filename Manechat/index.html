Rules

Welcome to SpectraLunar. Breaking the rules will result in being muted, kicked, or banned. Fundamentally.

  1. Be kind to other members. Don’t ping people unless you have a good reason to.
    1. Don’t interrupt people in voice chat. If people are trying to play a game, do not bother them.
    2. Properly calibrate your microphone. If your microphone is prone to environmental noise, enable Push to Talk or you will be muted.
  2. No offensive/controversial/nsfw avatars, pictures, emotes, or topics. This means no politics or religion.
    1. Links to other Discord servers must be verified by a Princess as SFW before being posted.
  3. All discussion, pictures, and any related content about an episode must be kept in the #spoilers channel until 72 hours after the official air date, even if it may have leaked earlier.
    1. Do not carelessly spoil important plot points of any media in general, regardless of it’s age. Intentionally spoiling anything is an immediate permaban.
  4. Shitposting, spamming, and low-effort memes are prohibited. Don’t use excessive unicode or emoji in usernames or text. Don’t use emotes more than four times as large as the subreddit emotes.
  5. DON'T JUDGE - We all have our preferences, sexual or otherwise, don't insult or ridicule someone for their likes and dislikes, you have your own kinks too..

    6. All Princess decisions are final.

    • Princesses have full discretion to remove content or users deemed unpleasant or unwelcomed by community members.
    • Princesses have full discretion to change channel topics.

  6. Anyone that comes from /r/mylittlepony discord server will be banned..

Respect Discord's Terms of Service.

Abusing or exploiting bugs in Ayana Bot or Discord itself is a bannable offense.

Certain channels are used for certain activities and discussions. Temporary channels may be created for certain events. Do not clutter the general chat with content that belongs somewhere else.

#chat General discussion.
#overflow General discussion channel #2 for when #chat becomes overcrowded.
#spoilers Spoilers channel to discuss new episodes, comics, movies, or any other restricted media.
#activities Gaming, Fanfic Reading reactions and TSSSF.
#announcements Important server announcements are posted here.
